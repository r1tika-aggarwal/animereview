RECENT REVIEWS

Jujutsu Kaisen

Yes, Gojo is the scene stealer in this anime but no lies every character and plot is just mind blowing, not an anime to miss watching.

Spy X Family

Want something light and cute and refreshing and funny to watch with slow burn romance and cool action scenes? THIS is your anime!

Demon Slayer

Might not grasp you in the first few episodes but as the story goes on will easily become one of your favourite animes.

Another

An intresting, mysterious and kinda creepy story, is short enough to finish in one go and is not something you'll regret watching.

Horimiya

One of the best romance anime i have watched, although CAUTION, you'll be falling hard for Miyamura, the male lead.

Haikyuu!

An anime which will make you fall in love with volleyball and you will find yourself rooting for these players more than for yourself.

ALL TIME FAVOURITE

Attack on Titan

The manga artist, Hajime Isayama, got the idea for the Titans when he encountered a drunk customer at a coffee shop he worked in. He was inspired by the inability to communicate and realised the most dangerous thing was the human race.

Ngl, for the first time in my life I am thankful to a drunk customer. Truly a splendid pience of art, as the story will progress your mind will get blown and blown.
